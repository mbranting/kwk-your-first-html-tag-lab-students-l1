LOCAL GIRL BECOMES AN INSPIRATION FOR OTHERS

We all dream of our minute of fame, but for this girl, she no longer needs to dream. Coco Waszak's recent voyage to Arizona from California for the inspirational women's Kode with Klossy camp, initiated by the beloved model Karlie Kloss, uncovered her prodigious coding skills that baffled even the director of the program herself.
Waskzak adds, "I didn't know what I was doing at the time, but I managed to hack into NASA. After realizing what I did, the trainers of the camp contacted Karlie Kloss herself!"

Little did Coco Waskzak understand what was going to happen. Karlie Kloss offered this young coder not only a free ride to one of the most prestigious college in the world, but also a chance for her to travel around the world with her to share her story. "I was scared that I was going to get in trouble at first," Waskzak describes, "But after realizing how impressed Karlie was, I knew something incredible was going to come from this." After the incident, NASA experts fixed their system to prevent this from occuring in the future, and offered Coco a job as a professional hacker.
